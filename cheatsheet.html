CheatSheet

For any class requiring a colour, replace:

  • red* with colour name needed - red, orange, amber, yellow, lime, green, emerald, teal, cyan, sky, blue, indigo, violet, purple, fuchsia, pink, rose, slate, gray, zinc, neutral, stone
  • 50* with colour step needed - 50, 100, 200, 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900, 950
